About RoBoVine Dairy Product

A lightweight, ergonomic delivery system that automates the cleaning, disinfecting, stimulation, drying, and post-dip treatment of dairy cow teats. Consistent handling of the cow teat along with the ability to customize water temperature and air pressure reduces cow stress and increases milk flow-rate.
